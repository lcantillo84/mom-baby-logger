# Privacy Policy for Baby Tracker

**Last Updated: November 14, 2024**

## Introduction

Baby Tracker ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our app handles your information.

## Data Collection

**We DO NOT collect any personal information.**

The Baby Tracker app:
- Does NOT collect any user data
- Does NOT transmit data to external servers
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking
- Does NOT require user accounts
- Does NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal information

## Data Storage

All data you enter into the app (feeding records, diaper changes, notes) is:
- Stored ONLY on your device
- Never transmitted to any server
- Never shared with third parties
- Completely private and under your control

## Data Backup

The app creates local backup files on your device to prevent data loss. These backups:
- Remain on your device only
- Are never uploaded to cloud services
- Are never accessible to us or any third party

## Data Export

When you use the "Export" feature:
- Data is copied to your device's clipboard
- You control where you paste this data (email, messaging apps, etc.)
- We do not have access to where or how you share this data

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

This app does NOT use any third-party services, including:
- No advertising networks
- No analytics services
- No crash reporting tools
- No cloud storage services

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:
1. Open the app
2. Go to Settings
3. Select "Delete Data"
4. Choose the timeframe to delete

Deleting the app from your device will also permanently remove all data.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored only on your device:
- You have complete control over your data
- You can export your data at any time
- You can delete your data at any time
- Your data cannot be accessed by anyone else

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app and on this page.
